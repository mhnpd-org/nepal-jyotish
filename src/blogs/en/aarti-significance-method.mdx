### How to Perform Aarti (Simple Steps)

Key points to observe when performing Aarti:

1. Recite the deity's root mantra three times after worship.
2. Offer flower offerings (Pushpanjali) three times.
3. Light the lamps (ghee or camphor) ‚Äî it is customary to use odd numbers (5, 7, 9...).
4. Perform Aarti while playing conch, bell, drum, and other instruments.

Sequence:

- Circle 4 times at the feet
- Circle 2 times at the navel / mid-section
- Circle 1 time at the face
- Circle 7 times covering the entire form

This symbolically offers light to the elemental centers of the body, ultimately surrendering the entire consciousness.
