### How to Perform Aarti (Step-by-Step)

Key points to observe while performing Aarti:

1. Recite the deity's main mantra (Mool Mantra) three times after completing the worship.  
2. Offer flower offerings (Pushpanjali) three times.  
3. Light lamps using ghee or camphor тАФ an odd number of wicks is considered auspicious (5, 7, 9тАж).  
4. Perform Aarti while ringing conch, bell, or playing other instruments.

### ЁЯМ╝ Conclusion

Aarti is not merely the conclusion of worshipтАФit is the illumination of devotion, the vibration of surrender, and the purification that dissolves imperfections into light. Performing, witnessing, and absorbing the experience of Aarti are all highly meritorious. Regular, disciplined Aarti enhances mental focus, creates a sattvic (pure) environment, strengthens devotion, and completes the spiritual experience of worship.
